Broadcast on January 13th, 1972, the David Frost show was the beginning of a series of live TV performances from John and Yoko. Often overlooked are the two David Peel songs that bookmark the show, with John playing a tea chest bass and Yoko playing bongo. The only official release of the show came in the form of a short intro for John Sinclair that appeared on John Lennon Anthology, however, the rest of the show has been archived.
