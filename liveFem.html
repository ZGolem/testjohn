The International Feminist Planning Conference was a feminist event held in Cambridge, Massachusetts from June 1st to 4th. On the second day of the conference, Yoko, backed by John on guitar, performed several songs. The full set is unknown, but prior to the performance, Yoko distributed a sixteen-page pamphlet containing the lyrics to 'Sisters, O Sisters', 'Looking Over From My Hotel Window', 'Approximately Infinite Universe', 'Coffin Car', 'Warrior Women', 'Now Or Never', 'Let Me Count The Ways', and 'Women Power'. On the 3rd, Yoko (with John on Bongos) led a frenzied performance of 'Woman Power'. The live performance of 'Coffin Car' appeared as a bonus track on Yoko's 'Feeling the Space' album.
