Give Peace A Chance Live

John and Yoko appeared at an anti-war protest march today and lead a crowd through a quick rendition of 'Give Peace a Chance.' Although pretty primitive, the performance here is remarkably similar to the performance seen later at the One to One concert and on the Jerry Lewis Telethon. John's speech and the runthrough of 'Give Peace a Chance' was recorded by a news broadcast and shown partially in the 2003 'Lennon Legend' DVD.
