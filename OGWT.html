Calling back to his 'Top of the Pops performance 5 year prior, John performed on the show 'The Grey Whistle Test'. He had previously recorded the interview segment of the show on March 11th, and today recorded a "live" performance of two oldies to promote his latest record 'Rock N Roll'.

The performance was released on both the 1992 'John Lennon Collection' VHS and 2003 'Lennon Legend' DVD. The show was broadcast on April 18th, with an uncut performance broadcast on the Australian network 'Rage' in 1986.
