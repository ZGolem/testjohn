During a week in mid-February (air date), John and Yoko co-hosted 5 episodes of the Mike Douglas show. Mike Douglas opened each show with a song, this time being the Rube Bloom song 'Don't Worry Bout Me'. John performed Imagine, and the episode ended with a performance by Vivian Reed. The fourth episode (third episode recorded) was broadcast on February 17th, despite not appearing on any CD release, the entire week worth of episodes was released on VHS in 1998.
