I'm Walkin' (for the March of Dimes) Live

The March of Dimes was an annual twenty-mile charity walk held in Central Park. The walk ended with a concert by the Chambers Brothers. John and Harry Nilsson made a brief apperance where they sang a few lines of the Fat's Domino song 'I'm Walkin', slightly modifying the lyrics. News footage was captured of the event.
